What is this competition ?


This is a platform to be the face of your school. Your work would be to promote RAZZMATAZZ and its events by increasing its reach people. The school ambassador programme aims to develop student leadership skills by providing oppurtunities for students.

What do we expect from the ambassadors ?


We expect him/her to promote the events, increase the like and shares of every post on the page and bring maximum number of students from their respective schools to RAZZMATAZZ.

On what basis the winners will be selected ?


The winner will be selected on the basis of their communication and leadership skills. The work and the action of each participant on the page would be kepttrack of(e.g. the like and share of every post of the page). It also depends on the personal interview which will be held during the event itself.

Is there any criteria for participating in this competition ?


All you need to be is a student of class 9 or above .
